MY WORK

A mobile application that serves an online platform for electrical services. The client will book a service from a selected service provider, it can be a company or an individual. The program also allow the user to choose an individual service provider, the system will also provide the service provider information such as years of experience and ratings. It has also the information of the bus destination and vice versa. The languages used to develop the system are PHP, JAVASCRIPT, and BOOTSTRAP while the database is MYSQLi.
